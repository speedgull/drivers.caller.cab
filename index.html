The Taxi Company That Works For You

CallerCab is different. We're proud to be a cooperative business. One that is owned and managed by the workers themselves, with no shareholders or investors taking a slice of the profits.

When you work with CallerCab you get to keep more of what you've earned.

Work when you want

You choose when you want to work. You can log into the system whenever you like and are free to accept or reject any jobs that are offered to you.

We'll provide you with all the statistics you need to help you decide the most profitable times for your car to be on the road.

What's more, you're not tied into lengthy contracts. You're free to leave and join the cooperative as often as you please.

Keep your existing customers

You've worked hard to build your business and you've probably got plenty of loyal customers that you'd like to retain.

Because CallerCab operates an online service that supplements your existing commitments, you're free to continue accepting direct bookings or work from the taxi ranks.

We can also help you manage your business account customers;

  • Dedicated booking portal
  • Specify which vehicles can be assigned account jobs
  • Custom tariffs
  • Integrated accounting and invoicing

Want to offer a more reliable and efficient service? We will be more than happy to help you integrate your customers into our booking system.

Jobs assigned fairly

Everyone is equal at CallerCab. Our app tracks vehicle locations using GPS and matches incoming jobs to the nearest available car. If two or more cars are a similar distance away then it is offered to whoever has waited longest.

Lost runs needn't mean lost earnings. If a customer doesn't show up you'll automatically get first refusal on the next available booking.

Long distance jobs are offered on a rotational basis with each car taking its turn.

Low fees and no commission

We don't have an expensive office staffed by overpaid managers - our business is conducted entirely online and democratically managed by our members.

CallerCab is essentially a booking agency, created to unite drivers who have had enough of being exploited by traditional taxi and private hire companies.

There are no hidden fees and we don't charge commission. You get to keep 100% of the fare, and we charge hackney carriage tariffs across our entire fleet. No discounts, no cheap fares, and no greedy businessmen benefiting from your hard work!

Each vehicle owner pays an equal share of the costs and in return receives an equal share of the work. What could be fairer than that?
